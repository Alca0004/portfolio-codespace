About Me

Hi, I’m Aaron Alcala, a front-end web developer with a passion for crafting clean, modern, and user-friendly websites. With a background in environmental services, hospitality, and customs, I bring a unique perspective to problem-solving and design. My journey into web development began with a curiosity for creating intuitive digital experiences, and it has grown into a commitment to building sleek and functional web applications. I specialize in HTML, CSS, and responsive web design, focusing on accessibility and performance. My goal is to create projects that are not only visually appealing but also efficient and easy to navigate. I’m currently refining my skills through a web development bootcamp, where I’m exploring new techniques to push my creativity further. This portfolio is a reflection of my journey and a space to showcase my work. Feel free to explore and reach out!
