About Me

Hi, I’m Aaron Alcalá — a front-end web developer passionate about building clean, responsive, and user-friendly interfaces. I started with HTML and CSS, and my journey has grown to include JavaScript, SCSS, API integration, and modular project architecture. With a background in environmental services, hospitality, and customs, I bring a grounded, problem-solving mindset and a strong eye for detail to every project. Whether it’s styling a layout from scratch or structuring an app with dynamic functionality, I enjoy turning ideas into interactive, accessible digital experiences. Currently enrolled in a full-time web development bootcamp, I’m continuously expanding my skills through hands-on projects that reflect real-world challenges. This portfolio captures my growth so far — and there’s a lot more to come. Thanks for stopping by. Feel free to explore my work or connect with me!
